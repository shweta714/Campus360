Help Center

Getting Started

How to Sign In

  1. Click the "Get Started" button on the home page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Sign In" to access your account
  5. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link below the sign-in form

Using Campus360

Navigation

  • Home: Return to the main page at any time
  • About: Learn more about Campus360's features and mission
  • Feedback: Share your experience or report issues
  • Help: Access this help guide

Key Features

  • Dashboard: View your courses, assignments, and upcoming exams
  • Profile: Update your personal information and settings
  • Courses: Access your enrolled courses and course materials
  • Assignments: Submit assignments and view grades
  • Exams: View exam schedule and access online exams

Common Issues

Login Problems

  • Make sure you're using the correct email address
  • Check if Caps Lock is turned on
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Use the "Forgot Password" option if needed

Need More Help?

If you can't find the answer to your question, please visit our Feedback page to contact support.
